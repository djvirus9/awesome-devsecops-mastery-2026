# KPI Definitions

## Core Metrics

- MTTA: mean time to acknowledge security alerts
- MTTR: mean time to remediate findings
- SLA compliance: % issues fixed within target SLA
- SBOM coverage: % releases with SBOM
- Signed artifact coverage: % releases with signatures
- Policy exception rate: exceptions per month

## Suggested Targets

- MTTA < 4 hours (high severity)
- MTTR < 14 days (high severity)
- SBOM coverage > 95%
- Signed artifacts > 95%
