# Vulnerability SLA Matrix

| Severity | Target Response | Target Fix | Example Sources |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Critical | 24 hours | 7 days | [CVSS v3.1](https://www.first.org/cvss/), [NVD](https://nvd.nist.gov/) |
| High | 48 hours | 14 days | [OWASP](https://owasp.org/) |
| Medium | 5 days | 30 days | Internal findings |
| Low | 10 days | 90 days | Internal findings |

## Guidance

- Use compensating controls for delayed fixes
- Track SLA compliance in a dashboard
- Require security approval for SLA exceptions
